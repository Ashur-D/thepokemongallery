The Pokémon Gallery

Gallery

This expansive image series explores the intersection of the Pokémon world and modern gaming culture. Each artwork features a different beloved Pokémon immersed in a video game setup tailored specifically to their personality and habitat. The collection spans a wide variety of gaming experiences, from Pikachu at a high-end PC battle station and Charizard lounging with a home console, to Squirtle enjoying a handheld by the pool and Lucario engaging in active VR. It extends to unique scenarios like Gengar playing retro horror in a haunted house, Machamp dominating an arcade fighter, and Alakazam using psychic powers to play holographic chess in a Zen garden. The series perfectly illustrates that there is a type of game—and a way to play—for every type of Pokémon.
